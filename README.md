# FMCG Profitability & Risk Analytics 

This project analyzes FMCG category-wise profitability and risk exposure using **SQL** and **Power BI**.

---

## Business Objective

- Identify high-risk categories with low profit margins  
- Track revenue contribution across regions  
- Provide action-based recommendations for management decision-making  

---

## Tools Used

- **SQL** (Data Preparation & Querying)  
- **Power BI** (Dashboard Design & KPI Tracking)  
- **DAX** (Profitability Measures & Risk Segmentation)
